Welcome to North Wales' Most Popular Car Boot Sale

Experience the best car boot sale in North Wales. Located in Rhyl Showfield, we offer quality sellers, amazing bargains, and a friendly community atmosphere every weekend.

Guidelines

Seller Guidelines

  • Setup only during designated hours
  • Payment required before setup
  • Must remain until 12:00 PM (weather dependent)
  • Follow staff instructions (Abuse towards staff will not be tolerated)
  • Courteous behavior required (Abuse towards other sellers will not be tolerated)
  • Household items only

Buyer Guidelines

  • Pay entry fees at gate
  • Get stamp for re-entry
  • Follow designated areas
  • Respect seller spaces
  • No "boot diving"
  • Cash only

Prohibited Items

  • Weapons or dangerous items
  • Stolen goods
  • Counterfeit items
  • Adult material
  • Live animals
  • Food/beverages

🐕‍🦺 Service Animal Support

We welcome service animals and their handlers. Our staff are trained to provide assistance and support for visitors with disabilities. If you need any accommodations or have questions about accessibility, please contact us in advance or speak to our staff on site.
